Biographical/Historical note

Established 1910 as Grace Lutheran Church by the Central District of the General Synod; became East Jefferson Evangelical Lutheran Church, 1911. In 1934 joined the Michigan District of the Missouri Synod and named Golgotha Lutheran Church. In 1955 it became Riverside Lutheran Church.
