Biographical/Historical note

Elizabeth Molnar's daughters were Emma and Margaret (Margit). Emma worked in the Diagnostic Clinic at Harper Hospital in the late 1940s, where many letters to her were sent. She earlier served in a WAC Detachment, HQ Command, Company "D", of the U.S. Army during World War II. Emma married Gustav Simon, of Lincoln Park, and the collection also contains a number of letters addressed to him.
