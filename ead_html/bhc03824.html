General Physical Description note (2 boxes, 1 wallet, 22 volumes)
Abstract Papers contain financial reports, advertising material, correspondence and patents for this Detroit music store. Also included are papers for the Cleveland Talking Machine Company, Grinnell Realty Company, and Shackelton Piano Company.
