Abstract
Research papers and associated materials based on correspondence between Arnheim and other auto enthusiasts. Covers the early history of automobile manufacture in Indiana, local history of Connersville, Ind., the McFarlan family history, history of the McFarlan Automobile Company and the McFarlan automobile, including early automobile racing with McFarlan racers. Notable correspondents include Larry M. Ackley, Don Baumgart, Henry Blommel, Thomas Burness, Henry Austin Clark, Jr., C. Ashton Cox, Phil Crummett, Alan Dashall, Menno Duerksen, Jeffrey Godshall, Peter Helck, Leslie R. Henry, Melvin Illa, Brooks Lozier, Dwight Marvin, John Neff, J.B. Nethercutt, Scott Newhall, John M. Peckham, Hugo Pfau, Frank Potter, Irving Silverman, Dr. Frederick Simeone and Ralph R. Teetor.

Preferred Citation

Alvin J. Arnheim Collection, National Automotive History Collection, Detroit Public Library.
