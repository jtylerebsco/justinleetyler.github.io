Abstract
This collection deals with the Stanton family of Grosse Ile, Mich. Most of the letters in the collection are addressed to Alexandrine (Macomb) Stanton; her sisters, Catherine (Macomb) Mason and Sarah (Macomb) Stanton Williams; and her stepson, Stephen Keyes Stanton.
