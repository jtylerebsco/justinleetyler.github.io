Creator: Ely, Heman.
Title: Heman Ely papers,
Dates: 1799-1886.
Quantity: 0.4 linear ft. (2 wallets)
Abstract: This collection deals with pioneer life in Ohio, concentrating on such matters as lands, turnpikes, mill stones, bells, founding of a college, establishment of counties, shipping conditions, Lorain Iron Company affairs and more. The land papers, c. 1799-1838, pertain to titles in the Connecticut Western Reserve, Mich., etc. Taken as a whole, the papers reflect the lives and interests of a Ohio pioneer, together with his Mass. and other eastern business and personal connections.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Heman Ely, originally from West Springfield, Mass., settled in Elyria, Lorain County, Ohio, circa 1818.
