Biographical/Historical note

Detroit Terminal Railroad Company was located originally in Highland Park, on Woodward Avenue, with a freight office on Kercheval in Detroit. It opened around 1914 as the Detroit Terminal Railway Company, and remained in business for over sixty years. By the 1970's it was located on Mound Rd.
