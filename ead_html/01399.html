Abstract
A collection of letters, photographs, family histories and genealogical charts of ten families related to the Keister family, some of whom were early settlers of Brownstone Township, Mich. Each family history goes back several generations. Major family groups are Keister, Knapp, Pattee, Strechbury (also spelled Strotsbury, Stretbury, Strechberry and Stretsberry), Hopkins, Noble, Lowe and Williams. Various spellings of Keister include Kester, Kiester, Keester, Koester, Kuster, Custer and Custard.
