Revision history:

Creator: Pantera, Grace
Title: Grace Pantera Papers,
Dates: 1864-1970
Quantity: 5.5 linear ft. 8 boxes 10 Volumes
Abstract: This is a genealogy collection compiled by Grace Leavitt Pantera. It contains materials mainly on the Leavitt, Kincaid and Rogers families among others.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection

Arrangement note

Series; Organized into two series: I: Biographical and personal material. II. Genealogy and related materials

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.

Restrictions

Restrictions: This collection is open for research use.
