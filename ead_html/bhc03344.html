General Physical Description note (14 boxes)
Abstract The collection includes plats of the City of Detroit and its environs showing industrial locations, railroad terminals and streets. Also included are The blueprints which of commercial and industrial buildings for sale or lease by the Lupton-Sherk Company.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Lupton-Sherk Company, business and real estate brokers. The company was established in the early 1930's by Ben Lupton and O. Allen Sherk and was located in downtown Detroit at 904 Guaranty Building.
