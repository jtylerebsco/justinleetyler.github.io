Scope and Contents note

Letters to his wife "Lizzie" and her replies during his assignment as Ass't Adjt. Gen. U.S. Vols on Gen. A. S. William's Staff. Captured twice. Also his children's correspondence, "Mollie", Ross, and Charles. Includes photo albums.
