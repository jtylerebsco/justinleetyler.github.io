Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Creator: Pryor, Helen Rudy
Title: Helen Rudy Pryor papers,
Dates: 1960-1984.
Quantity: 21 linear ft. 21 linear ft. (42 boxes)
Abstract: Genealogical notes on many families including Wallingford, Ruggles, Rudy, and Seaman families. Many census notes particularly from Kentucky censuses on these families.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and the world wide web.

Restrictions: This collection is open for research use.
