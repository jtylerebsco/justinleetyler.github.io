Biographical/Historical note

Mary McLennan Streit was born in Bay City, Michigan on January 14, 1904 or 1905. She graduated from the University of Wisconsin, and became a teacher of English at the International Institute in Detroit. She and her husband, Dr. William F. Streit, a Detroit dentist, were married on June 5, 1929. They had two daughters, Joanne and Gretchen. Dr. Streit died in 1962. Mrs. Streit became involved in politics during the 1950's, and was a delegate to the State Republican convention. She served as vice-chairman of the Republican State Central Committee in 1952. She was president of the Cottage Hospital Auxiliary, 19831985, and was active in numerous other organizations, including the Daughters of the American Revolution, Women's Historical Club and First Presbyterian Church. Mary M. Streit lived in Harper Woods until her death on August 14, 1988.
