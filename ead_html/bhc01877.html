Biographical/Historical note

Olive Elizabeth Mudge was born in Nankin Township, Mich. in Mar. 1893, the daughter of W.C. and Elmira M. Mudge. Her father worked as a railroad section laborer. She had a young sister, Frances, who was born in 1894. Miss. Mudge graduated from the University of Michigan Training School for Nurses in 1917, and soon afterward joined the U.S. Army as a nurse, serving during what would be known as World War I. She was stationed at the U.S. Army Post Hospital at Fort Sill, OK. before being sent to France.
