Biographical/Historical note

Founded on January 11, 1906 for the purpose of the culture and enjoyment of the fine arts in Detroit, the society sponsors musical, artistic and dramatic events. The group promotes the association of both professional and non-professional artists in the furtherance of all fine art activities, with musical concerts, art exhibits, theatrical presentations and a chamber music series.
