Finding Aid for Papers of Alvin J. Arnheim, 1964-1972.


Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Creator: Arnheim, Alvin J.
Title: Papers of Alvin J. Arnheim,
Dates: 1964-1972.
Quantity: 13 boxes.
Quantity: 6.5 linear ft.
Abstract: Research papers and associated materials based on correspondence between Arnheim and other auto enthusiasts. Covers the early history of automobile manufacture in Indiana, local history of Connersville, Ind., the McFarlan family history, history of the McFarlan Automobile Company and the McFarlan automobile, including early automobile racing with McFarlan racers. Notable correspondents include Larry M. Ackley, Don Baumgart, Henry Blommel, Thomas Burness, Henry Austin Clark, Jr., C. Ashton Cox, Phil Crummett, Alan Dashall, Menno Duerksen, Jeffrey Godshall, Peter Helck, Leslie R. Henry, Melvin Illa, Brooks Lozier, Dwight Marvin, John Neff, J.B. Nethercutt, Scott Newhall, John M. Peckham, Hugo Pfau, Frank Potter, Irving Silverman, Dr. Frederick Simeone and Ralph R. Teetor.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. National Automotive History Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Automobile enthusiast.

Arrangement note

Organized into the following series: I. Correspondence; II. Book manuscript and correspondence related to book orders; III. History of Indiana, Cornersvill, Ind., and of the McFarlan Automobile Company; IV. General automobile specifications, including the McFarlan, 1910-1928, filed by year, model and series; V. Non McFarlan materials.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid available in the National Automotive History Collection and on the World Wide Web.
