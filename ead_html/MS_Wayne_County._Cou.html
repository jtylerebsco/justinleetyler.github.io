Abstract
Though the majority of records in this collection are from the Wayne County Circuit Court, there are other courts represented: Justice Court, Macomb and Washtenaw Counties Circuit Courts; Police Court, Probate Court for Wayne and Oakland Counties; Recorders and Superior Courts of Detroit. William Look was the attorney who handled most of the above cases. Records from his law office make up this collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Born in Detroit in 1857, William Look was the son of Arnold and Catherine Canto Look. His education was largely self-aquired. He learned much as a boy in the offices of his uncle, Judge Joseph Kuhn, and later read law in a number of prominent law offices. Look was admitted to the bar in 1880 and in 1885 was chosen a member of the Board of Councilmen, the upper house of the Municiple Legislature. His election to the Wayne County Circuit bench, on which he served one term, took place in 1887. He married Christine Audretsch in 1897 and they had five children. William Look died in Detroit in 1934.
