Abstract
Includes correspondence, registers, treasurers' records, clerks registers, record books, records of subscriptions to Baptist periodicals for church members, catalogue of members, sunday school records, minutes and records of the Board of Deacons, Board of Trustees, General Efficiency Committee, and related groups including the Women's Union, W.A.S. (Welcome All Strangers) Society, Woman's Mission Circle, Ladies Benevolent Society, Ladies Aid Society, and Women Aid society. Also includes records for Green Ave. Baptist Church and North Baptist Church.
