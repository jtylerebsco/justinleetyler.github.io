Creator: Blair, Austin, 1818-1894.
Title: Austin Blair papers,
Title: 1838-1921.
Quantity: 32 linear ft. (64 boxes, 2 wallets, 4 microfilm reels).
Abstract: Chiefly papers of military and political interest, including some letters relating to the impeachment of Andrew Johnson.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Governor of Michigan, 1861-64, state legislator and U.S. congressman, 1866-1870.
