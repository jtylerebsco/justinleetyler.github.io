Title: Stanton Family papers
Dates: 1793-1923.
Quantity: 5.5 linear ft.
General Physical Description note (11 boxes, 2 large manuscripts)
Abstract This collection deals with the Stanton family of Grosse Ile, Mich. Most of the letters in the collection are addressed to Alexandrine (Macomb) Stanton; her sisters, Catherine (Macomb) Mason and Sarah (Macomb) Stanton Williams; and her stepson, Stephen Keyes Stanton.

Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
