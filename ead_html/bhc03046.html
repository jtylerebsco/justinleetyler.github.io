Biographical/Historical note

Pi Lambda Theta is a national honor and professional association for women in education.,In May 1922, a petition was drawn up asking for an alumnae chapter in Detroit, and the first regular meeting under the new charter was held one month later.

Scope and Contents note

Papers include minutes, reports, newsletters and organizational papers.

Arrangement

Organized into three series: I. Organizational papers (box 1-6). II. Programs and projects (box 7). III. Printed materials (box 8-9).
