Biographical/Historical note

Dr. Oliver Fiske, the son of Nathan and Sarah (Hill) Fiske, was born in Brookfield, Mass. in 1762. He served in the Revolutionary War and was graduated at Harvard University in 1787. He married Sarah Duncan in 1796, and practiced medicine in Worcester, Mass., where he was also active in civic life. Oliver Fiske died in Boston in 1837.

Scope and Contents note

This collection contains mainly family letters of the Fiske and Gay families of Hingham, Mass.
