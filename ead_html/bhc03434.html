General Physical Description note (20 boxes, 1 extra large manuscript)
Abstract Speeches, festival programs, publicity, photographs and records of festival chairpersons and committee members.

Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Coordinates various festivals for the ethnic groups in Detroit, Mich.

Arrangement note

Organized into the following series: I. Festival chairmen and committee member records, 1971-1982; II. Cultural and steering committee records, 1978-1984; III. Promotional notebooks; IV. Media notes, speeches, publicity and background information; V. Ethnic festival programs, 1974-1984; VI. Festival photographs, 1970-1982; VII. Press releases and clippings, 1972-1985.

Other finding aids note
