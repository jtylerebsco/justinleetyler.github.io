Biographical/Historical note

This Protestant Episcopal hospital was erected in 1868.

Scope and Contents note

Includes: will (copy) of Matilda R. Canniff bequeathing funds to build hospital; abstract of title; daily record books (1873-1924; journals, 1881-1923; ledgers (1911-1924); letterbook (1911-1929).
