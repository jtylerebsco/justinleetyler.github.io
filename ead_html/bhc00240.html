Title: Boston-Edison Protective Association records,
Dates: 1926-1983.
Quantity: 5 linear ft .(10 boxes, 1 large manuscript)
Abstract: Correspondence, articles of incorporation, bylaws, minutes, membership rosters, and material relating to violations of restrictions and legal cases regarding the zoning restrictions in the historic district within Boston, Edison, and other streets.
