Series I: Personal Papers
Scope and Contents note
Photographs with actor Alan Hale, Jimmy Durante, Arnold Palmer, Governor and Mrs. Milliken, poet Ogden Nash, G. Mennen Williams and Pierre Salinger are contained with personal papers (Box 1:5 and 1:7). Also included in personal papers is correspondence from President Richard Nixon, Vice-President Hubert Humphrey, Michigan Governor William Milliken, George Romney and Ohio Governor Adlai Stevenson (Box 1:2).

Series II: Correspondence
Scope and Contents note
Mr. Arnett's columns drew thousands of comments from his readers, and he saved them all. Many prominent people featured in these responses, including letters from Detroit mayors Roman Gribbs and Coleman Young and Michigan governors G. Mennen Williams, George Romney, John B. Swainson and William Milliken.
