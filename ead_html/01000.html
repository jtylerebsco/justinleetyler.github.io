Collection Inventory

Series I: Personal / Professional Papers 

Scope and Contents note

As a certified genealogist, Mrs. Giltner had interests both personally and professionally concerning her field. These papers refer to her own family as well as those of her clients. Charlotte Giltner and her husband, Justin, had a family home in Leland, Michigan. Materials on Leland are also included here.
