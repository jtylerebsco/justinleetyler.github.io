Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Founded in 1909 in America, the Daughters of the British Empire is an organization open to any woman of British or Commonwealth birth or origin (as far back as a grandparent) and is now a resident and/or citizen of the United States.
