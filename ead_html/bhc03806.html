General Physical Description note (3 boxes)
Abstract This collection contains letters written by Private Norman H. Schnell to his wife, Beatrice, during World War II. The letters were written from Fort Mead. Maryland, then from England, France and Belgium. There are also a number of photocopies of V-Mail letters.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Schnell, a member of the 480th AAA Anti-Aircraft Battalion, Battery C, was from Bay City and Essexville, Michigan.
