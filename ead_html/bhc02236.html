General Physical Description note (4 boxes)
Abstract Includes letters and material about the Rider, Symons, and Smart families of Saginaw, Mich. Ebenezer W. Rider (1849-1913) was an attorney, inventor and businessman involved with Rider Mfg. Co., Rider Quick-Adjusting Handle Bar, and Rider Heater Co.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
