Abstract
The files include concert programs from 1927 to 2006 and biographies, publicity and notes on the music for some musicians. Meeting minutes, committee reports and correspondence are in the recent folders.

Biographical/Historical note

A Detroit chapter of the national Pro Musica organization, which booked European composers to perform their own works in the United States, was formed in 1927. After the national organization folded, the Detroit group decided to go forward, booking the artists and making the concert arrangements. Each year three programs are presented, including composers and new artists who are on the threshold of becoming widely recognized. The 38 composers and hundreds of artists appearing for this unique concert society have ranked consistently among the finest in the world.
