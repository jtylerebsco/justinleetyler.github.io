Biographical/Historical note

The brothers' last name appears to have been originally Kaylor, but by 1942 both boys were called Bassford.

Return to Table of Contents »

Scope and Contents note

Collection consists mainly of letters written from brothers Allen and Lawrence Bassford during World War II to their mother, Mrs. Hattie Kaylor of Hopkins, Michigan.
