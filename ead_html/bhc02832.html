Abstract This collection contains various papers concerning fur and merchandise trade between Detroit and Montreal and other eastern cities.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Thomas Williams was an early Detroit merchant and father of John R. Williams, Detroit's first mayor.

Arrangement note

Organized into three series: I. Boxes. II. Daybooks. III. Petty letters;each series organized chronologically.
