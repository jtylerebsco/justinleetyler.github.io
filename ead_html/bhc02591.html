Biographical/Historical note

Born in Richmond, NY on Oct. 20, 1820, Robert P. Toms moved with his family to Troy, Mich. in 1831. He came to Detroit in 1847 to study law. He was trustee of Elmwood Cemetery, 1878-1884, St. Luke's Hospital, and treasurer of the Detroit Bar Association, 1881-1884. He died in Detroit on Mar. 10, 1884.
