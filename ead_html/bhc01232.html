Biographical/Historical note

Arthur L. Holmes was a Detroit businessman.

Scope and Contents note

This collection contains correspondence and other papers which relate to Holmes' activities as secretary of the Michigan Retail Lumber Dealers Association. A small portion concern domestic and family affairs.
