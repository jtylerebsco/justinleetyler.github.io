General Physical Description note

Abstract
Materials include bank correspondence, 1918, 1926, and legal papers, many concerning various properties in the 1920s-30s Detroit, Grosse Pointe Farms and Royal Oak. Some personal items, doing from 1913, include Sweeney's law school diploma from Detroit College of Law, 1914, and his acceptance as an attorney by the Supreme Court of Michigan.
