General Physical Description note (3 boxes)
Abstract Papers include minutes of meetings, newspaper clippings, publications and correspondence. Many of the papers are photocopies, but there are a number of original documents in the collection.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

The American Polish Engineering Association is an affiliate member of the Engineering Society of Detroit's Affiliate Council. The purpose of the organization is to unite engineers, scientists and other persons of Polish heritage who are interested in such technical matters.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
