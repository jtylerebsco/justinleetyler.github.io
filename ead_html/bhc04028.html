General Physical Description note (2 boxes, 1 volume, 1 large manuscript)
Abstract Materials include bank correspondence, 1918, 1926, and legal papers, many concerning various properties in the 1920s-30s Detroit, Grosse Pointe Farms and Royal Oak. Some personal items, doing from 1913, include Sweeney's law school diploma from Detroit College of Law, 1914, and his acceptance as an attorney by the Supreme Court of Michigan.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Donald N. Sweeney was a vice-president of Peoples State Bank in Detroit.
