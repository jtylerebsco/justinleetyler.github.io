Biographical/Historical note

Born April 2, 1904 in Detroit, Michigan. Haviland Reves was a professional writer who wrote on a variety of topics concerning Detroit trade from air cargo through plumbing and the zoo. He was also a genealogist who wrote the "Reves (Reeves) Family".
