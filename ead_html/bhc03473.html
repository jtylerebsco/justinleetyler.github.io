General Physical Description note (9 boxes, 1 volume)
Abstract This collection contains papers belonging to Mrs. Helveston, all relating to education in general and the Detroit Public Schools in particular. It gives a good overview of schools and education in Detroit in the 1970's.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Mary Anne M. Helveston was a Detroit Public School Board Member of Region Five.

Biographical/Historical note

Region Five schools were on Detroit's northwest side, including Mumford and Central High schools, and many elementary and junior high schools in those neighborhoods.

Arrangement note

Arranged into two series: I. Subjects. II. Meetings.

Series II: Meetings
Scope and Contents note
Mrs. Helveston attended numerous meetings, all dealing in one way or another with the schools. Minutes of meetings, mainly for Region Five Board of Education, are contained here. Materials on various committees are included as a subseries.
