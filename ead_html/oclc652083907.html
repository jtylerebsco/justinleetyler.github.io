Biographical/Historical note

The University District Citizens Association is a neighborhood association that monitors the University District of Detroit. The University District is a neighborhood that lies between 7 Mile Road and 6 Mile (McNichols) Road with Livernois and Parkside streets as east and west boundaries. The University District Citizens Association is comprised of University District residents on a volunteer basis and provides many services for the University District including arranging social functions and snow removal services.
