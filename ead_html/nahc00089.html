Finding Aid for James V. Higgins papers, 1972-1993.


Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Creator: Higgins, James V.
Title: James V. Higgins papers,
Dates: 1972-1993.
Quantity: 8.4017 linear ft. (9 boxes)
Abstract: Collection of press releases, photographs (of professional grade and represent executives, facilities, and vehicles/products).and other material generated by public relations departments of automobile manufacturers and related industries. Working files of James V. Higgins, automotive journalist for the Detroit News. Represents product lines, company direction and issues and concerns of the industry in general.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. National Automotive History Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Journalist and columnist for the Detroit News, James V. Higgins covered the automotive industry for over four decades.

Arrangement note

The original files came complete with an inventory for each box provided by Mr. Higgins. These inventories contained descriptions that ranged from the title of the folder alone, to lengthy descriptions of the items in the folder and their relevance. Due to the physical size of the original files and the wealth of material contained in them, when necessary, the original files have been arranged in smaller groupings arranged by chronology and type of material, while respecting the original order (and themes this order represented) as much as possible. Each box retains a copy of Mr. Higgins' original inventory within.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid available in the National Automotive History Collection and on the World Wide Web.
