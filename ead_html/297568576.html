Biographical/Historical note

Sergeant, U.S. Army (1946-1948); Wayne County Prosecutor's Office, Wayne County Sheriff; Mayor of Detroit (1970-1973); Wayne County 3rd Judicial Circuit Judge (1974-1983); Justice of the Michigan Court of Appeals (1983-2000)

Return to Table of Contents »

Scope and Contents note

This collection contains the official records and correspondence of the Gribbs Administration, from 1970-1973. It includes general files, subject files and miscellaneous files kept by his office. Materials are arranged alphabetically within the sections.
