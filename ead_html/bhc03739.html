General Physical Description note (7 boxes, 2 volumes)
Abstract This collection includes correspondence, manuscripts, newspaper clippings and subject files of this Detroit teacher and writer. The majority of the material concerns sports and the Thumb District of Michigan.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
