Biographical/Historical note

Cass Technical High School began as Cass Union School in 1861 in Detroit, Mich. The school is located on a triangle of land bounded by Second Ave., Grand River Ave. and High Street. The Music Dept. was formed in 1907 and has had many notable graduates including trumpet player Donald Byrd and violinist Regina Carter.
