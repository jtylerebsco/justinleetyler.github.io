Biographical/Historical note

Catherine Abbott was a descendant of artist James Whistler. She married George Thomas Abrey and they had a son, Thomas Abbott Abrey. Both husband and son died in 1918, Thomas at age 23 while serving in World War I. Catherine died in 1955.
