Biographical/Historical note

Detroit Adventure, a coordinating arts council, was founded in 1959 to strengthen the cultural activities of its member institutions. Up to twenty-six cultural and educational institutions comprised Detroit Adventure.

Participating Institutions 

General note

Participating Institutions—also known as "Delegates"—were the members of Detroit Adventure. This series contains material on all the participating institutions, as well as affiliate members.

Programs 

General note

The organization's calendar of events is included in this series, along with the many programs and projects it sponsored. Among the more notable were: Conversations in the Arts, Day in the Arts, poetry workshops, Excursions in Music, May (Arts) Odyssey, Noel Night, and the American Revolution Bicentennial. Also contained in this series are Audio-Visual Materials. More information on programs may be found in Volumes: Scrapbooks and Loose-leaf Binders.
