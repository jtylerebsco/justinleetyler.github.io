Biographical/Historical note

Adelaide Canfield was born Sept. 7, 1890. Her father, George Lewis Canfield (1866-1928) had joined his father's (Frank H. Canfield) Detroit law practice, which specialized in admiralty law, in 1892. The Canfields merged with Michigan's oldest law firm in 1914, creating Miller, Smith, Canfield, Paddock and Perry. Adelaide's mother, Mary Noble Croul (b. Feb. 9, 1865) married George on Sept. 25, 1889. They also had a son, William M. Croul Canfield (b. Dec. 21, 1891.) Adelaide married Julian Ballenger Hester, Jr. in Asheville, North Carolina in Jan. 1927. They lived in Tyron, N.C. Their daughter, Mary Adelaide Hester, was born in Atlanta, c. 1931. Adelaide was a charter member of the Detroit Junior league and was active in charitable and social welfare work in Tyron. Adelaide died in 1970 in North Carolina. She is buried in Elmwood Cemetery in Detroit. Mary died in March 2007 in Spartanburg, N.C. and is buried in Elmwood Cemetery.
