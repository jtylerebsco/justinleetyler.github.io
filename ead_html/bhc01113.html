General Physical Description note (3 boxes, 2 large manuscripts, 4 microfilm reels)
Abstract Papers contain originals and photostats of correspondence, accounts, military papers, family genealogies and other items relating to the settlement of the estate of Hamtramck's first wife and to Hamtramck's private business enterprises.
