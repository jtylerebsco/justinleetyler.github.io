Biographical/Historical note

Edith V. Alvord was president of the Michigan State Federation of Woman's Clubs for many years. She was also a member of the YWCA Metropolitan Board. She and Dr. William Alvord, a Detroit dentist, were married, and their daughter, Fran Harris, was a noted radio newscaster in the Detroit area.
