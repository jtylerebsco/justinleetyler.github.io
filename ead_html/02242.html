Abstract
Contains minutes and papers of the Bohemian club (1890-1905); miscellaneous papers on Miami School (Detroit) including class rolls and 2 photographs; correspondence on English Speaking Union Fellowship, and personal notebooks of poems and prose.

Return to Table of Contents »

Biographical/Historical note

Detroit teacher; corresponding secretary of the English Speaking Union; founded the Riggs Scholarship which was designed to bring English students to Ann Arbor and send American students to England. She was secretary of the Bohemian Club.
