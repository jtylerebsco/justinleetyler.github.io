Title: Detroit Rotary Club Records
Dates: 1910-1999.
Quantity: 26.0 Linear feet(44 boxes, 1 large manuscript, 14 volumes, 1 film reel (16 mm), 3 film reels, (8 mm), 2 sound cassettes, 3 sound tape reels)
Location note Level A, Level B, B6m
Language:
Language of Materials In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Detroit Rotary began in 1910 with a membership of 15. By 1940 membership was over 400.

Scope and Contents note

Papers, newsletters, correspondence and other materials. Formats include film and sound tapes, audio cassettes and slides.

Arrangement

Organized into the following series: I. Background and history; II. Board of directors; III. Members; IV. Projects and programs; V. Public relations.

Restrictions on Access: This collection is open for research use.
