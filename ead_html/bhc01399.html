Finding Aid for Dorothy Keister papers, 1848-1989.


Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Creator: Keister, Dorothy.
Title: Dorothy Keister papers,
Dates: 1848-1989.
Quantity: 2 linear ft. (4 boxes, 1 large manuscript).
Abstract: A collection of letters, photographs, family histories and genealogical charts of ten families related to the Keister family, some of whom were early settlers of Brownstone Township, Mich. Each family history goes back several generations. Major family groups are Keister, Knapp, Pattee, Strechbury (also spelled Strotsbury, Stretbury, Strechberry and Stretsberry), Hopkins, Noble, Lowe and Williams. Various spellings of Keister include Kester, Kiester, Keester, Koester, Kuster, Custer and Custard.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Dorothy Keister is the daughter of Cecile Williams Keister who compiled this collection.

Arrangement note

alphabetical order by family surname.
