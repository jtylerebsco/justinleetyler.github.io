Biographical/Historical note

Born on Slocum Island (now Elizabeth Park), Detroit, October 17, 1883. Son of James Benton Nichols and Elizabeth Slocum. Graduate in last from U. of M. 1908. Entered investment brokerage, major in world war I. Married Anne Donnelly 1917, had children; Elliottt S. Jr., Elizabeth Truax, Anne Donnelly, Charlotte Donnelly. Died at his home in Bloomfield Hills April 7, 1959. (from Burton. History Wayne County v. 3, P. 364-5) (blue index cards)
