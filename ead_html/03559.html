General Physical Description note

Abstract
Correspondence, abstract of title, bibliographies, research notes, charts, clippings, monographs, legal briefs, ordinances, oral interviews, cassettes, histories, maps, surveys, proposals, ethnic statistics, and photos, relating to the Poletown section of Detroit, Polish Americans, General Motors Corporation, Immaculate Conception Catholic Church, Central Industrial Park, and Hamtramck, Mich.
