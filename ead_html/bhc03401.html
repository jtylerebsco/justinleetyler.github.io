Title: Grace Pantera papers,
Dates: 1864-1970
Quantity: 5.5 linear ft.
General Physical Description note (8 boxes, 10 volumes)
Abstract This is a genealogy collection compiled by Grace Leavitt Pantera. It contains mainly materials on the Leavitt, Kincaid and Rogers families.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Arrangement note

Organized into two series: I. Biographical and personal material. II. Genealogy and research materials.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.

Restrictions

Restrictions: This collection is open for research use.
