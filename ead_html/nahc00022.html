Biographical/Historical note

Architect; graduate of the University of Michigan Architectural School; designed remodeling plans for the Ford Motor Company's Ford Rotunda in 1952. Also designed the Hazel Park Raceway clubhouse.

Finding aid and white index cards available in the National Automotive History Collection and on the World Wide Web.
