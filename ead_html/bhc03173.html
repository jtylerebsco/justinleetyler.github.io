Scope and Contents note

This collection contains a large number of materials on Michigan public men and measures, many of them of decided interest. Included are eight Thanksgiving proclamations, 1826-1834 and 1873-1878. Also included are numerous petitions and memorials to the Territorial and State legislatures and to the Governors of Michigan requesting organizations of towns, appointments of particular men to office and dealing with other public matters. Reports of Douglass Houghton on the geological survey of Michigan in th 1830s, and of the State Board entrusted with the Michigan exhibition at the Philadelphia Centennial Exposition in the 1870's are contained in the collection.
