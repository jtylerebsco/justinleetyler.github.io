General Physical Description note
(65 boxes, 154 volumes, 1 large manuscript)
Location note
A21n
Abstract
This collection includes letter books, journals, ledgers and letters of several members of the Lothrop family. Papers of George Lothrop also contain cash books, day books and other papers relating to his law career.
