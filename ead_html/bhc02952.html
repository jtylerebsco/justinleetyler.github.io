Biographical/Historical note

Sidney B. Morris was born at Detroit in 1896. He operated the S. B. Morris Co., a metal brokerage firm, and was active in this field for 35 years. He was a member of Temple Seth El, Union Lodge of Strict Observance No. 3, F. and A. M., and the United Commercial Travelers. Mr. Morris died May 1, 1956. (from Detroit News, May 1, 1956) (Blue index cards)
