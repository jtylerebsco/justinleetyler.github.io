Abstract
This collection consists of correspondence to, from and concerning juveniles who had contact with the Wayne County Juvenile Court, Detroit, Michigan, from 1908-1929. Medical correspondence includes 1934 only. Autographs of a number of prominent figures may be found in this collection, including: Michigan Governor Fred M. Warner (Box 1:5); Detroit Mayor Philip Breitmeyer (1:6); Michigan Governor Woodbridge N. Ferris (4:9; 5:9; 7:2; 16:2); D.B. Duffield (2:11); and U.S. Senator James Couzens (16:2).
