Biographical/Historical note

The Loomis Family Papers contain photographs, genealogy and correspondence of a pioneering family from the Ionia, Michigan area. Frances Loomis was a Detroit Public Library librarian from 1919-1951. The papers of her family extend from an 1833 Psalm Book owned by her namesake, grandmother Mary Frances Perkins Loomis, up to photocopies of Miss Loomis personal papers dated 1965.
