Scope and Contents note

Lists of funerals performed by Rev. Keightley, a circuit rider at Alamo, Mich., 1888-90; and New Baltimore, 1893. Also, list of marriages performed in Ingham, Mecosta and Kalamazoo counties, and Alamo, Mich., as well as marriage licenses for Allegan, Kalamazoo, Wayne and Macomb counties.
