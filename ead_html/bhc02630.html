Biographical/Historical note

Born in Lincoln, England in 1885. Mr. Udale came to America in 1921. He was a patent attorney for Holley Carburetor for 40 years. He was a member of the Engineering Society of Detroit, the Detroit Bar Association and a life member of the American Bar Association. (Detroit News, June 19, 1964) (blue index cards)

Scope and Contents note

Personal and business papers of Stanley M. Udale papers related to his law practice.
