General Physical Description note (2 boxes)
Abstract This collection contains materials for McCarthy's 1968 presidential campaign. Contained in the collection are biographical/personal material, campaign material, correspondence, McCarthy's stand on various Issues, public relations material and supporters, including celebrities such as Dustin Hoffman and Paul Newman.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
