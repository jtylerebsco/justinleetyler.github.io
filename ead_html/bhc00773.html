Biographical/Historical note

The George W. Drennan Company was originally formed under the name of Drennan & Sill, Inc., and its ownership than consisted of George W. Drennan, Charles H. Sill, Sheldon L. Drennan and Eugene R. Fortner. At later dates both Mr. Fortner and Mr. Sill withdrew. This company was formed for the purpose of carrying on a general real estate business, such as brokering, managing, negotiating loans and appraising of real estate.

Biographical/Historical note

The Drennan Realty Company was formed to take title to numerous parcels, principally vacant lots and other real estate which passed to Mr. Drennan at the time he severed his connection with the Alter Land Company.
