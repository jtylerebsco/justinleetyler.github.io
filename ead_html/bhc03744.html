Biographical/Historical note

William L. and John A. Wiederhold were born to Peter and Mary A. Wiederhold. They had an older brother, Cecil, who was a vocalist in Detroit in the 1920s. Their father was vice-president of Detroit Farm Products Company and they made their home on Detroit's east side. William was born on February 1, 1902 and married Ethel who was born the same year. They both died in Grosse Pointe, he in June 1982; she in August 1984. As a young man, William was an axle works inspector, an assistant manager for the Detroit Amusement Company and later, as a salesman for a printing company. William was a sales representative for Press Sign Company of St. Louis, Missouri, and American Sign Company of Cincinnati, Ohio, Detroit office. His brother John worked for a short time as a stenographer, before becoming a freelance photographer by the late 1920s. In 1946 William and Ethel became the owners of Burbank Apartment Building in Detroit, which they owned until 1963.
