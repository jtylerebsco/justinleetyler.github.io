Title: Canadian Archives,
Dates: 1659-1839.
Quantity: 1 linear ft. (2 boxes, 2 large manuscripts, 28 volumes)
Abstract: Papers contain appointments, orders and various other materials relating to British activities in Canada, including land grants and land surveys, Montreal notarial records, and Prerogative Court records.
Language: In (predominately) French and English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
