Biographical/Historical note

The Metropolitan Detroit Musicians League was originally founded in 1927 as the Wayne County Chapter of the Michigan Music Teachers Association. It later became the Detroit Musicians League. By 1997, due to the movement of members of the northern areas of the city, the name was changed to Metropolitan Detroit Musicians League.

Scope and Contents note

Papers include minutes, yearbooks and treasurer's reports.
