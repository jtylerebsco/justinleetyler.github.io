Biographical/Historical note

Virginia F. Paddock was a Detroit socialite in the 1920s. Active in the Detroit Junior League Players and Glee Club, she was also an avid golfer and a member of the Women's District Coif Association of Detroit. Her father was Lewis Henry Paddock, a prominent Detroit attorney.

Scope and Contents note

Scrapbooks, photographs and letters of Detroit socialite Virginia Paddock, daughter of prominent Detroit attorney Lewis H. Paddock. Miss Paddock was an avid golfer and active in the Detroit Junior League during the 1920s.
