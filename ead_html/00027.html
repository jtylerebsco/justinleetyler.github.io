Abstract
This collection contains legal and business papers of Thomas Allen and Lewis Allen; correspondence and papers relating to the estate of Julia Larned Allen and Sylvia Allen; papers of Larned, Allen, and Tefft families. Two of the bound volumes are letter books of John G. Atterbury for the years 1841-1849, when he was a Detroit attorney and land agent for Thomas Allen.
