Title: Woolfenden family papers,
Dates: 1870-1995.
Quantity: 1 linear ft. (2 boxes)
Abstract: This branch of the Woolfenden family originates with Justin and Mary Rice. This collection begins with their daughter, Ruth, who married Francis Raymond. They had eight children, among them Francis (Frank) Jr., Harriet and Ida, who are featured in this collection. Another daughter, Ella, married Frederick Woolfenden. Ella and Frederick had four children, including Frederick Woolfenden II. Frederick II married Clara Mackie and they had three children: Agnes, Frederick (Fred) and William (Billy). Items of Fred and Billy are also included in this collection.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Series I: Genealogy, Letters and Personal/Family Items
Scope and Content note
The earliest items in the collection are letters dating from 1817 and 1818, to Laura Rice and Nancy Raymond. Also included here are remarkably well-preserved Civil War letters written by Frank Raymond Jr., of the 24th Michigan Infantry.
