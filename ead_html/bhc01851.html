Biographical/Historical note

Charles Moran was the eldest son of Claude Charles and Marie Ann (Belleperche) Moran, born March 29, 1755. On December 9, 1775, Claude was slain by his brother-in-law, Joseph Hecker, who was hanged on the Detroit public common. Charles, on November 4, 1794, married Catherine Vessiêre dit Laferté, daughter of Louis and Catherine (Esprit dit Champagne) Vessière dit Laferté. They had two sons named Charles, one of whom died in infancy. Charles Sr. was captain of the Northeast Coast Militia. He was buried, October 6, 1815; Catherine was buried, November 3, 1797. (from B.H.C. Leaflet, III, 12-13; IV, 40-42) (blue index cards)
