Title: Detroit Institute of Musical Arts records,
Dates: 1914-1976.
Quantity: 1.3 linear ft. (1 box, 2 volumes, 1 large manuscript)
Abstract: The papers of this Detroit music school include minutes, building construction plans and blueprints, and legal papers. It also contains minutes of the Detroit Musical Settlement School, which later became the Detroit Community Music School
Language: In English.
