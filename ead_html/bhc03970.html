Title: Incorporated Society of Irish-American Lawyers records
Dates: 1962-2009.
Quantity: 2 linear ft. (2 boxes, 3 volumes, 1 large manuscript)
Abstract: The collection consists of meeting minutes, agendas, the organizations constitution and bylaws, articles of incorporation, correspondence, event programs, membership rosters, financial records, newsletters and three volumes of newspaper clippings and photographs. There is also a copy of the book the Michigan chapter put together called A history of the Incorporated Society of Irish-American Lawyers as well as a VHS cassette called Pictorial History of Irish-American Lawyers.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

The Incorporated Society of Irish-American Lawyers is an organization where members must be judges or lawyers with Irish ancestry. The ISIAL began in 1978 and was still active in 2009.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
