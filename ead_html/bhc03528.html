Biographical/Historical note

Henry Green was born in Chicago on January 11, 1904 and grew up in Gary, Indiana. He moved with his family to Detroit some years later. He graduated from the University of Michigan Dental School in 1929 and was married a year later to Edna Sklar. Dr. Green's dental office was located on Detroit's east side for the entire fifty-one years of his practice. He was very active in a number of organizations, both professional and personal. He served as historian to both the Detroit District Dental Society and the Alpha Omega Dental Fraternity, of which he served as that organization's first Jewish president. He was also a member of the Michigan State Dental Association and was president of the Jewish Historical Society of Michigan from 1975-1977. Dr. Henry Green died on February 1, 1982.
