Abstract
This collection contains correspondence and other papers Trowbridge's legal career, his service with the 10th Michigan Calvary during the Civil War, and his residence in Knoxville, Tennessee and Detroit.

Biographical/Historical note

General Luther S. Trowbridge was born in Oakland County, Michigan. He attended Yale University in 1857, but left college before graduation to devote himself to a legal career. He served as Colonel of the Michigan Cavalry, 1862-65, and from 1856 to 1868 resided at Knoxville, Tennessee. He came to Detroit in 1868.
