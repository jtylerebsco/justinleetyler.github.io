General Physical Description note

Abstract
Contained in this collection are letters, minutes of meetings and reports of the various clubs. Autographed letters from Detroit Mayor Albert Cobo and Michigan Governor George Romney are also included.

Biographical/Historical note

Lena (Mrs. David) Zuhlke served as president of the Detroit Federation of Women's Clubs and was a member of several other Detroit area organizations.
