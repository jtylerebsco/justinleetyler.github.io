Biographical/Historical note

John Mullett and his son, James H. Mullett, were government surveyors in Michigan, Indiana, Illinois, Wisconsin and Arkansas.Born in Halifax, Vt., July 11, 1786; moved with father's family to Genesee Co., N.Y., ca. 1806; went to Buffalo, engaged in tailoring; moved with his family to Detroit, 1820; was appointed surveyor of government lands for aprox. 30 years, surveying large portions of Mich., Ill. and Indiana; one of founders of Detroit (Masonic ) Lodge) 1821; Grand Master,1844-45; removed to Ingham Co., Mich., 1854; died Jan. 13, 1862. (from Conover, Free masonry in Mich., v. 1, p. 256-268) (blue index cards)
