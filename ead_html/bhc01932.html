Creator: Norton, John,Jr.
Title: John Norton papers,
Dates: 1835-1866.
Quantity: 2.5 linear ft. (5 boxes)
Abstract: This group of papers pertains to the affairs of the Michigan State Bank; to the services of Norton and James A. Armstrong as cashiers, and F.H.Stevens, G.F. Porter, and C.C.Trowbridge as presidents; financial affairs of S. T.Mason; Allegan County development; affairs of various Michigan banks; legal correspondence of Bates,Walker and Douglass.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Cashier of the Bank of Michigan.Caine from Albany,N.Y., in 1835 to be cashier of Michigan State Bank. In 1839 his place was taken by James A. Armstrong.
