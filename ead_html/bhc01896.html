Biographical/Historical note

Detroit banker; also Detroit treasurer, controller, and postmaster.Born September 11, 1873 father from Westphalia, Germany. Deputy Collector of Internal Revenue-Cleveland Admin. Chief bookkeeper-City Treasurer. Deputy City Treasurer. Deputy City Controller, 1907. Postmaster of city of Detroit, 1913-1922. City Controller, 1923. Managing Director of Liquor Control Commission, 1933. President of Grosse Pointe Saving Bank. During World War 1. U.S. Army Quartermaster Corp. As Postmaster-renumbered the streets of Detroit. Amateur boxer and baseball player. Member of Detroit Yacht Club. Treasurer of Detroit Yacht Club for two years. Funeral August 24, 1954. Daughter, Dorothy. (B.H.C. Biog. File) (blue index cards)
