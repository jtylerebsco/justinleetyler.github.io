General Physical Description note
(5 boxes, 15 volumes)
Abstract
The collection includes minutes of meetings, scrapbooks, membership directories and correspondence of the group. The oldest items are newspaper clippings in scrapbooks dating from 1929.

Biographical/Historical note

The Woman's National Farm and Garden Association was organized in June 1926. The Detroit Branch of the organization began January 30, 1931.
