Biographical/Historical note

Gersham Mott Williams was born in Fort Hamilton, New York. He was the son of Thomas and Mary Bailey Williams. (Papers of his father, Thomas, are also contained in the Burton Historical Collection.) G. Mott Williams became Bishop of First Episcopal Church of Marquette, Michigan. The manuscript collection contains memorandum books, scrapbooks, diary, record of marriages at Holy Trinity, St. Matthew and St. George churches in Detroit, 1884-1887; some letters of the John Biddle family while they lived in Europe (1954-1861), and of Mrs. Susan Dayton (Ogden) Biddle, 1874.
