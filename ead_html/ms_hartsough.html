Biographical/Historical note

Palmer Hartsough was a native of Redford, Wayne County, Michigan, though he lived most of his life in Plymouth. Born on May 7, 1844 to Wells and Thankful Palmer Hartsough, he became a music teacher as a young man, and a prodigious writer of religious songs. Late in life he became a Baptist minister, and for twelve years conducted the Baptist church at Ontario, Wisconsin.
