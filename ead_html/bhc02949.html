Biographical/Historical note

James Stanton Park was born in Buffalo, New York on November 5, 1860. He came to Detroit in 1865 with his parents. He graduated from high school in 1878 and married Harriet Clarke in 1897. He was a poet and author in Detroit. (from Detroit Saturday Might, April 19, 1930 James Stanton Park Papers) (blue index cards)
