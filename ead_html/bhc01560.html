Biographical/Historical note

Thomas Spencer and Emily Lloyd were a well-respected Detroit family. They had three children: Arthur Henry, Bertha E., and Ethel Spencer Lloyd, who was a music teacher in Detroit. Ethel died on October 25, 1968.
