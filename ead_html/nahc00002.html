Repository: Detroit Public Library. National Automotive History Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Born in Nottingham, England on July 10, 1874, Cave came to the U.S. in 1900 and worked as an engineer on both electric and internal combustion engine cars. He worked for the Electric Vehicle Co. on the Selden patent car. He also worked as an engineer for the American Locomotive Co. and later was president of Cave Welding Co. At the end of his career he was director of development of the Fuller Brush Co. His numerous patents include designs for warfare materials, brushes, and electrical appliances.
