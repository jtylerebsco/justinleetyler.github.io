Title: Allen A. Warsen papers,
Dates: 1882-1980.
Quantity: 2 linear ft. (2 boxes, 1 volumes, 1 large manuscript).
Abstract: Papers include newspaper clippings, photographs, writings and correspondence pertaining to Detroit's Jewish history. Also included in the collection are oral history tapes of Rabbi Ernst Conrad, 1976-1977.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Allen A. Warsen was chairman of the Jewish Community Center's Yiddish Culture Committee and a local historian on Jewish culture.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
