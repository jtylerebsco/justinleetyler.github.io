Title: Department of Public Works records,
Dates: 1926-1970.
Quantity: 25.2 linear ft.
General Physical Description note (8 boxes, 1 wallet, 70 volumes, 10 extra large manuscripts)
Abstract Files from the Bureau of Highways; Expressways include plans, blueprints, reports and correspondence for Detroit expressways, particularly the Jeffries Freeway, and also the Davison, Chrysler and M-53 Freeways.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Arrangement note

Organized into two series: I. Office Files. II. Expressways and Freeways.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
