Biographical/Historical note

The Jim Dandy Ski Club, founded in 1958, is the oldest African-American Ski Club in the world. It was founded by Frank Blount, William Morgan and Reginald Wilson.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
