General Physical Description note (13 boxes)
Abstract This collection contains mostly scrapbook items and other papers of the Blain family, and Dr. Donald Blain in particular, such as medical and school materials. The earliest items in the collection appear to be photographs and photocopies of early family materials; the earliest dated items include scrapbook materials, a National Geographic Magazine and a book published in 1924. The most recent items include e-mails from the 21st century.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Donald Gray Blain was the grandson of Alexander W. Blain, landscape architect and designer of Detroit's Elmwood Cemetery. A Civil War Veteran, Mr. Blain served as Head of Parks and Boulevards for the City of Detroit and superintendent of Elmwood Cemetery. Donald Blain was the son of Dr. Alexander W. Blain jr., surgeon and founder of Alexander Blain Hospital and Clinic. Donald Blain was born in 1924 and became a doctor, specializing in Urology. He married Grace Carpenter and they had three children, Ian, Tricia and Betsy.
