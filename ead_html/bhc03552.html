General Physical Description note (4 boxes)
Abstract Correspondence, articles, lectures, poems, sketches, illustrations, certificates, programs, exhibition catalogs, passports, photos, and miscellaneous items, relating to Witold Watha (1884-1967) and Catherine Szendzikowska (1890-1961) Kosicki; their children, Bohdan Watha (b. 1925), George Jaroslaw (b. 1928), Witold Lubomir (b. 1922), Sigmund (Zbigniew) Watha (b. 1920), and Slawomir Watha (1924-1970) Kosicki, and Rosalia Dobromila (Kosicki) Richards (b. 1930) and Mira (Kosicki) Walilko (b. 1916); Polish Technology Society, Detroit, Mich.; and J.T. Wing & Co., Detroit, Mich., maker of metal alloy products.
Language:
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
