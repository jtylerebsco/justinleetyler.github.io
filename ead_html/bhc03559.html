Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Title: Poletown history project,
Dates: 1919-1982.
Quantity: 3.5 linear ft. (7 boxes, 1 extra large manuscript)
Abstract: Correspondence, abstract of title, bibliographies, research notes, charts, clippings, monographs, legal briefs, ordinances, oral interviews, cassettes, histories, maps, surveys, proposals, ethnic statistics, and photos, relating to the Poletown section of Detroit, Polish Americans, General Motors Corporation, Immaculate Conception Catholic Church, Central Industrial Park, and Hamtramck, Mich.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
