Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

James M. Babcock served as a chairman of the Republican State Central Committee and delegate for the Gratiot County Republican Party.

Arrangement note

Organized into two series: I: General Office Files. II: Campaign Material
