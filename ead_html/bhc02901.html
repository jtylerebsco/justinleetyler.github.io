Biographical/Historical note

John B. Yates entered service in Co. A, Mich. Engineers and Mechanics as captain, Aug. 22, 1861 from Albion, Mich.; commissioned, Sept. 12, 1861; mustered, Oct. 29, 1861; commissioned major, May 28, 1863; mustered, June 16, 1863; commissioned colonel, Nov. 4, 1864; mustered, June 8, 1865; mustered out in Nashville, Tenn., Sept. 22, 1865. (from Heitman) (blue index cards)
