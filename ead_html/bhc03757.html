General Physical Description note (1 box)
Abstract Collection of letters, receipts and accounts concerning land deals in and around Detroit, St. Mary's Falls Ship Canal Co. and Toledo, Oh. It includes the records of Houghton & Hubbard (later, Houghton, Hubbard & Hubbard and Houghton, Hubbard & Welles) from 1836 through 1860, with accounts by Thomas H. and Henry G. Hubbard. Noted cartographer Bela Hubbard's signature also appears in this collection, as does that of Michigan geologist Douglass Houghton. Other records include those of Stephen W. Palmer of Norvel and Napolion, Michigan, with much correspondence with Jacob Ten Eyck of Cazenovia, Madison County, N.Y.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
