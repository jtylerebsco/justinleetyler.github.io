Finding Aid for Georgian Bay Line records,1812-1971


Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Creator: Chicago, Duluth and Georgian Bay Transit Company.
Title: Georgian Bay Line records,
Dates: 1812-1971
Quantity: 84 linear ft.(84 boxes, 107 volumes, 8 extra large manuscripts)
Abstract: The Chicago, Duluth & Georgian Bay Steamship Line, commonly known as the Georgian Bay Line, ran passenger cruises through the Great Lakes and the St. Lawrence Seaway. This collection contains the records, reports, blueprints and other historical documents pertaining to the company.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Arrangement note

Series:Organized into five series: I: Correspondence. II: Accounting and Finance Department. III: Passenger Department. IV: Shipping Department. V: Vice-President's Files

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
