Biographical/Historical note

Born in St. Johns, New Brunswick on October 25, 18,58 to Alexander and Martha Nevers. he was a teacher until her marriage to James Arthur, a photographer, in 1882. She care to Detroit with her husband and became involved in the equal suffrage movement in 1885. Mrs. Arthur was involved in child labor groups and was a member of the board that built the Detroit Tuberculosis Sanatorium. Arthur died in Detroit on October 26, 1929. (from Detroit News, October 31, 1929) (blue index cards)
