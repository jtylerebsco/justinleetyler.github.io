Finding Aid for Women's Economic Club of Detroit records, 1962-1993.


Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Creator: Women's Economic Club of Detroit.
Title: Women's Economic Club of Detroit records,
Dates: 1962-1993.
Quantity: 18.8 linear ft. (34 boxes, 6 volumes, 1 large manuscript).
Abstract: Primarily board of director meeting minutes, finance reports, committee materials and publicity. Also includes biographies, photographs and autographs of prominent people who were speakers, such as Dr. Joyce Brothers, the Reverend Jesse Jackson, and local politicians.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Created in 1962 with 23 businesswomen and now includes more than 1000 women from the professional and corporate worlds.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
