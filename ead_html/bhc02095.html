Biographical/Historical note

Early settler in Montrose township, Genesee Co., Michigan; established first store in October, 1867; was school inspector and superintendent of schools, justice of the peace, deputy county surveyor. Was in business with Philetus S. Church in Detour, Michigan, before settling in Montrose. (from Hist. of Genesee Co., Mich. p. 443-5) (blue index cards)
