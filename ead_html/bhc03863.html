General Physical Description note (5 boxes, 7 volumes)

Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

The Detroit Symphony League was originally established in 1939 as the Junior Women's Association for the Detroit Symphony Orchestra. The purpose of the organization is to support the Detroit Symphony through its many fundraising projects.

Arrangement note

Organized into three series: I. Projects. II. Presidents' Papers. III. Organization Papers

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
