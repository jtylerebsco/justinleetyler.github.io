Finding Aid for Lothrop family papers, 1843-1918.


Finding aid created by: Justin Lee Tyler
Date:

Revision history:

Title: Lothrop family papers,
Dates: 1843-1918.
Quantity: 72 linear ft. (65 boxes, 154 volumes, 1 large manuscript)
Abstract: This collection includes letter books, journals, ledgers and letters of several members of the Lothrop family. Papers of George Lothrop also contain cash books, day books and other papers relating to his law career.
Location: A21n
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.

Restrictions

Restrictions: This collection is open for research use.
