Revision history:

Creator: Stout Air Lines.
Title: Stout Airlines records,
Dates: 1918-1951.
Quantity: 11 linear ft. (22 boxes, 1 large manuscript)
Abstract: Included are letters, pictures, reports and bulletins of Stout Metal Airplane Co., and Stout Air Services Inc and information about early airplane building and air traffic.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Pioneer air lines organized by William Bushnell Stout in 1926.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and the World Wide Web.
