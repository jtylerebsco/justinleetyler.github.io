Dates: 1926-1961
Quantity: 1 linear ft. (2 boxes)
Abstract: Collection consists mainly of letters written from brothers Allen and Lawrence Bassford during World War II to their mother, Mrs. Hattie Kaylor of Hopkins, Michigan.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

The brothers' last name appears to have been originally Kaylor, but by 1942 both boys were called Bassford.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
