Title: Ward family papers,
Dates: 1807-1945.
Quantity: 1.0 linear ft.
General Physical Description note (2 boxes, 2 microfilm reels)
Abstract Papers include letters of Eber Ward, Eber Brock Ward, Emily Ward and others, concerning life at Conneaut, Ohio, Marine City, Michigan and Bois Blanc. It also contains material on shipbuilding, commerce and fishing, and Civil War letters of Asa Ward Brindle, who served in the 24th Michigan Volunteers. A portion of the collection appears on microfilm.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
