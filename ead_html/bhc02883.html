Revision history:

Creator: Woodbridge, William,1780-1861.
Title: William Woodbridge papers,
Dates: 1763-1919.
Quantity: 56 linear ft.(94 boxes, 29 wallets, 34 volumes, 12 large manuscripts, 1 microfilm reel)
Abstract: Correspondence, documents, family, legal and business papers, speeches and writings; account books (1805-1857); cash books (1813-1821); claim books (1804-1821); ledgers (1819-1822); index to appointments and resignations, Michigan Territory (1813-1826); Detroit Customs House reports (1802-1827); cash book (1824-1826); day books (1816-1828); import book (1815-1823); account book of the Library of Congress (1843).
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

Attorney from Marietta, Ohio who came to Detroit in 1814 as Collector of Customs. Held various positions in the Michigan Territorial government and was governor of Michigan, 1840-1841, and U.S. Senator, 1841-1847.
