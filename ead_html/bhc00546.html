Biographical/Historical note

Colony Town Club was founded in 1935 to further the educational, social, cultural and philanthropic interests of its members. For nine years the Club was located at 7850 East Jefferson Avenue. The Club House was closed in 1944 when the house, which Colony Town Club had occupied on a lease, was sold. Since 1947 Colony Town Club chief philanthropic project is the Cancer Loan Closet. In response to a plea from the American Cancer Society, the Club voted to established and maintain the Loan Closet at the headquarters on John R Street, now under the Michigan Cancer Foundation.

Biographical/Historical note

Colony Town Club's Cancer Loan Closet has become a model for other such Loan Closets throughout the country. Concerned in no way with attempted cures, the Cancer Loan Closet is devoted solely to providing aid and comfort to cancer patients. The Cancer Loan Closet is incorporated in the State of Michigan as the Cancer Loan Closet Foundation of Greater Detroit.
