Title: Russel family papers,
Dates: 1899-1949.
Quantity: 1.4 linear ft. (2 boxes, 2 volumes).
Abstract: Collection includes memorials to George H. Russel and his son George Bagg Russel, as well as other family photographs and diaries.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

George H. Russel was president of the Peoples State Bank, Chairman of the Detroit Clearing House Assn., and former president of the American Bankers' Assn.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
