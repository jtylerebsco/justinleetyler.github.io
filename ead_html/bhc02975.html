Biographical/Historical note

German singing society founded in 1849.The Harmonie Society was formed in 1849 in Detroit by Stephan, Frederic and John Marx and Gottlieb Frey because of their love of song and singing together. In 1856 the original constitution and by laws were revised an there were then 125 members. A club house was proposed and the cornerstone was laid October 22, 1874. The original club house was destroyed by fire in 1893 but a new club house was built and opened December 26, 1895. The Society celebrated its 125th anniversary in 1974. (from E & M 74D4 780.6 H2)
