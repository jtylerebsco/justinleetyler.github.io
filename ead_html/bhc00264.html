General Physical Description note (2 boxes, 3 volumes)
Abstract Business records of paint supply firm of P. Brady & Co., Detroit, Mich. Collection consists of a letterbook, journal, salesbook, bank books and check stub books of the Detroit firm of Preston Brady (1844-1909) dealing in paints and related supplies.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
