Scope and Contents note

Papers of Charles Andrew Cooley (1850-1922), electrical engineer, inventor, realtor, writer of poetry and newspaper articles; of his daughter Marjorie Louise Cooley (b. 1887), writer of children's stories, poetry and genealogy; and other members of the Cooley family of Detroit. Includes material related to Joseph Labadie, the Michigan Authors' Association, and inventors.
