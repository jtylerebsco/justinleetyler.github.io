Biographical/Historical note

The eldest child of Solomon and Sarah (Whipple) Sibley, Ebenezer S. Sibley was born in Marietta, Ohio in 1803.He attended a preparatory school in Clinton, New York, and graduated from West Point at the head of his class in 1827. In May 1831 he married Harriet L. Hunt, daughter of Judge Hunt of Washington. Sibley served in the Black Hawk, Mexican and Civil Wars and attained the rank of colonel in June 1861. He resigned from the army in April 1864, and died in Detroit on August 13, 1884.
