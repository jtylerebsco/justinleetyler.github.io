Biographical/Historical note

This archive came about as a collaborative effort of the Burton Historical Collection and the Gaelic League of Detroit, with the full cooperation of the United Irish Societies of Detroit. The organizational and individual contributions that make up this collection were solicited starting in 1997, with items being stored at the Burton Historical Collection. Starting in September, 2002, volunteers from the Irish organizations worked to sort, categorize, and assemble the collection in the form outlined in this Finding Aide.

Arrangement note

Series;Organized into four series: I. General history. II. Irish Organizations of Detroit and Michigan. III. Individuals. IV. Other material.

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.
