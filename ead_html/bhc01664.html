Biographical/Historical note

Ernest Timothy Marshall, known for his efforts to secure equal employment opportunities for minorities, was the first Director of the Department of Equal Employment and Contract Relations for the Detroit Public Schools. (blue index cards)

Scope and Contents note

Biography, correspondence and publications relating to the Detroit Public Schools - Department of Equal Employment and Contract Relations.
