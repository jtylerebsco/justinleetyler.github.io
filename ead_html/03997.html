General Physical Description note
(8 boxes, 1 wallet, 70 volumes, 10 extra large manuscripts)
Abstract
Files from the Bureau of Highways & Expressways include plans, blueprints, reports and correspondence for Detroit expressways, particularly the Jeffries Freeway, and also the Davison, Chrysler and M-53 Freeways.
