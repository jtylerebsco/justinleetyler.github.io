Biographical/Historical note

Born in Detroit on March 28, 1886, George Pare was the son of Maxim and Mary Lambert Pare. Ordained in 1909 at St. Patrick's Church in Detroit, Father Pare served as chaplain at St. Mary's Hospital; administrator of St. Joseph's Church in Erie, Michigan; assistant pastor of Our Lady of Help Church; chaplain at Providence Hospital; and United States Army Chaplain during World War I. His History of Detroit's Archdiocese, "The Catholic Church in Detroit, 1701-1888" won the 1951 John Gilmary Shea Award of the American Catholic Historical Association. Father George Pare died in Detroit in July, 1968.
