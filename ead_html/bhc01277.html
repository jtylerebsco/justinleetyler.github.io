General Physical Description note (2 boxes, 3 large manuscript)
Abstract Papers deal with Indian affairs and the government of Michigan Territory, including petitions of inhabitants of Detroit concerning land distribution; military orders; War of 1812; Hull's court martial, and his private land holdings.
