General Physical Description note (4 boxes, 3 volumes)
Abstract This genealogy collection includes legal documents, correspondence and photographs of the Neville and Crowther families. Scrapbooks contain postcards, newspaper clippings and greeting cards.

Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.
