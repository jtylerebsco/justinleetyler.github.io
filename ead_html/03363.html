Biographical/Historical note

The Woodbridge Citizens' District Council was formed in 1968 to support the rehabilitation of the Woodbridge area of Detroit, bounded by the New York Central Railroad tracks, Ford Freeway, Trumbull Avenue and Grand River Ave. The organization carried on research and other studies to develop an overall plan to assist in the economic, physical and social rehabilitation of the neighborhood.
