General Physical Description note (7 boxes, 6 volumes, 1 wallet)
Abstract This collection contains Minutes, Officers' Reports, Publicity and Scrapbooks of the organization. It also contains the papers of artist John Vogt.

Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

The GPAA was founded in 1938.
