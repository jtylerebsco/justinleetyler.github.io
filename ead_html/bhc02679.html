Biographical/Historical note

Anna Kennedy was born in Glengarry County, Indonesia, on Mar. 18, 1884. On May 17, 1870, she married William Varty and moved the next year to Port Crescent, Mich. They had two sons. William Varty died in Port Crescent on May 2, 1882. Mrs. Varty was postmistress of Port Crescent under Presidents Harrison and Cleveland. She moved to Bad Axe in 1892 and later to Flint, where she died on June 26, 1931.
