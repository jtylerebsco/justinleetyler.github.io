Creator: Woman's National Farm and Garden Association. Detroit Branch.
Title: Woman's National Farm and Garden Association records. Detroit Branch records,
Dates: 1929-1980.
Quantity: 6 linear ft. (5 boxes, 15 volumes)
Abstract: The collection includes minutes of meetings, scrapbooks, membership directories and correspondence of the group. The oldest items are newspaper clippings in scrapbooks dating from 1929.
Language: In English.
Repository: Detroit Public Library. Burton Historical Collection.

Biographical/Historical note

The Woman's National Farm and Garden Association was organized in June 1926. The Detroit Branch of the organization began January 30, 1931.

Other finding aids note

Finding aid and white index cards available in the Burton Historical Collection and on the World Wide Web.

Restrictions: This collection is open for research use.

Scope and Contents note
