Biographical/Historical note

Theodore Romeyn was born in Hackensack, N.J. on Aug 22, 1810. he studied law at Rutgers University, and was admitted to the New York Bar in 1832. Romeyn moved to Detroit in December 1835, where he lived until 1848 when he went to New York for ten years. He practiced law with Alexander D. Fraser in Detroit. Romeyn married Anna Mills Woodruff of Albany on Dec. 3, 1834; they had three children. Theodore Romeyn died in Detroit on July 22, 1885.
